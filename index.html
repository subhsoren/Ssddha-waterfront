About Us

Hello! Welcome to Siddha Waterfront.

Siddha Waterfront is a sprawling lavish residential offering beside a natural lake. With a modern and robust high rise that provides vibrantly happy living spaces for more than 1000 families. It doesn’t end there, with a clubhouse of more than 30,000 Sq Ft area waterfront offers world-class amenities and facilities. Located on the banks of the Hooghly river, Khardah with schools, public transport, hospital and markets being within arms reach.

Siddha Waterfront offers plenty of benefits that includes prime location, comfortable and lavish lifestyle, great amenities, healthy surroundings and high return.

Emerging as a self-contained community with 10 towers (G+14) comprising more than 1000 apartments, landscaped gardens, indoor games, swimming pool, gymnasium and children’s play area. Siddha Waterfront is an interconnected and secure community with 24/7 security and is smart home enabled.
